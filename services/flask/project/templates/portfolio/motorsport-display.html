  This product has been made for the customer: KMS

The digital motorsport display project was undertaken in collaboration with van Kronenburg Motormanagement Systems in Geldrop, Netherlands. The initial prototype was in an early stage of development, with the primary objective being to prepare the product for production. This involved extensive work across various disciplines to ensure a robust and reliable end product.





Key product specifications include:

  • Ability to display CAN data from the ECU on both a 'Check Engine Page' and a 'Race Page'.
  • Three buttons for menu navigation, with an additional three provided for vehicle installation.
  • Sixteen configurable LEDs for warnings when engine data exceeds set thresholds.
  • Sixteen RPM LEDs to indicate optimal gear shift points based on engine RPM.
  • Integrated GPS module for lap-timer functionality, allowing users to track performance on any EU circuit.
  • Ambient light sensor for automatic adjustment of LED and screen brightness.




The product utilizes a Raspberry Pi Compute Module 4 as the main processor. This decision was based on the prototype's existing architecture and the need for a future-proof solution within budget constraints. A custom carrier PCB, designed under our supervision by an external company, integrates all sensors and actuators with the Raspberry Pi.





The product's encasing, designed to be CNC routable, features a 800x400px display with 1000 nits of brightness for optimal readability in bright conditions. The screen is protected by a glass screen protector to prevent damage (not seen in the pictures).



The development of this digital motorsport display underscores our commitment to innovation and quality. By focusing on user-friendly design, robust hardware, and advanced functionality, we ensure that our products meet the high standards expected by professional rally and circuit drivers. This project exemplifies our ability to deliver customized, high-performance solutions that enhance the driving experience and provide crucial real-time data. Our dedication to excellence in both hardware and software makes us a trusted partner in the motorsport industry.
